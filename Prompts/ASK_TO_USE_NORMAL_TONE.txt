(example: “Pending Verification｜Annual Complaint Rate”).

Unit Standardization:

Time: 24-hour format, accurate to the minute  
Ingredients: percentage or grams per serving

No vague descriptions  
(e.g., “fast service” → must state “average serving time 4.5 minutes”).
